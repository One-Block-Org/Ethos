# Ethos Brand Identity

Ethos is a professional, high-performance transaction profiling suite for the Ethereum ecosystem. Its visual identity reflects **clarity**, **depth**, and **precision**.

## Primary Identity: The Professional Ethereum Flame
![Ethos Final Logo](/home/dean/.gemini/antigravity/brain/632434df-6fb5-4afa-95e4-9f8ff02150ca/ethos_logo_final_v5_clean_fire_1774400875860.png)

- **Vision**: A high-fidelity, professional-grade logo featuring a realistic yet stylized sapphire-to-cyan flame. The flame sits cleanly on the flat top of the iconic lower Ethereum diamond.
- **Color Palette**: Radiant Cyan, Deep Sapphire, Navy.
- **Message**: "Performance built on the Ethereum foundation."

## Visual Guidelines
- **Typography**: Primary font is **Inter** or **Roboto Mono**.
- **Tone**: Professional, local-first, privacy-conscious.
